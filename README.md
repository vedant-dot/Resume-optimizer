Resume Optimizer AI is an intelligent, AI-driven tool that helps you create job-winning resumes in minutes. It analyzes your resume against any job description and gives you clear, actionable suggestions to improve your chances of getting shortlisted.

## Overview  
Resume Optimizer AI lets you upload your resume, paste a job description, and instantly receive a tailored resume, a match score, and interview preparation material. It is designed for job seekers who want a fast, structured, and data-driven way to improve their applications.

## Key Features  
- **Resume Analysis** – Compare your resume against a job description and identify gaps in skills, keywords, and experience alignment.  
- **Tailored Resume Generation** – Automatically rewrite and adapt your resume using role-specific keywords and phrasing while keeping your original experience intact.  
- **Fit Score Checker** – Get a numeric score that shows how well your resume matches the job description and what to improve.  
- **Interview Readiness Analyzer** – Receive 5 technical and 5 behavioral questions with suggested answer guidance to help you prepare for interviews confidently.

## Tech Stack  
- **Python** – Core application logic and integrations.  
- **Streamlit** – Interactive, user-friendly web interface.  
- **Mistral LLM (via OpenAI-compatible API)** – Natural language understanding and smart content generation.  
- **PyMuPDF** – Robust PDF parsing to extract resume text accurately.  
- **ReportLab** – Dynamic PDF creation for exporting polished, updated resumes.
